Our Story of Dedication, Excellence, and Success

Our Story

Rays Academy was not just founded, it was built on a foundation of passion and a clear vision. Our journey began in 2006, when our founder, **Mr. Shankar Lal Amulani**, a veteran Mathematics faculty with decades of teaching expertise, decided to create a learning space that went beyond traditional coaching. His goal was simple yet profound: to provide a holistic and nurturing environment where every student could unlock their full potential.

For years, Mr. Amulani's dedication and results-oriented approach have shaped the academic journeys of countless students. He instills not just subject knowledge but also discipline, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills. Under his leadership, Rays Academy has consistently delivered a **100% success rate** and become a trusted name for quality education in Bhopal.

Our story is one of continuous growth, built on the trust of students and parents alike. We believe in personal attention, consistent support, and a commitment to excellence that prepares students for success not just in exams, but in life.
